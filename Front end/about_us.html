We are Trident!



Trident Services Private Limited incorporated in 2004 is promoted by the three Ex-General Managers of Cummins India Limited after having worked with the Cummins for 23 years. Trident commenced its operation as an Authorized Cummins Dealer for the District of Pune in June’2004. Additional area of Satara, Kolhapur, Sangli, Ratnagiri & Sindhudurg Districts in the state of Maharashtra was added into our operational territory by Cummins India Ltd in 2009. The company has its Head Office in PUNE and branch offices at Kolhapur, Satara, Chiplun & Main Parts Distribution Centre as well as Service Centre for repairs & Overhauls of Engine & Alternators at Shivane Industrial Estate. The Company is also an Authorized Dealer for Valvoline Cummins Limited for lubricating oil for engines and industry. The Company is also an Authorized Direct Dealer of Allison Transmission, USA for Automatic Transmissions.
Trident is an ISO 9001: 2008 certified & CRSIL Certified Company .
